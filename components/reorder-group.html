Usage‚Äã

<!-- The reorder gesture is disabled by default, enable it to drag and drop items -->
<bkkr-reorder-group disabled="false">

  <bkkr-item>
    <bkkr-label>
      Item 1

    <bkkr-reorder slot="end"></bkkr-reorder>
  </bkkr-item>

  <bkkr-item>
    <bkkr-label>
      Item 2
    </bkkr-label>
    <bkkr-reorder slot="end"></bkkr-reorder>
  </bkkr-item>

  <!-- Default reorder icon, start aligned items -->
  <bkkr-item>
    <bkkr-reorder slot="start"></bkkr-reorder>
    <bkkr-label>
      Item 3
    </bkkr-label>
  </bkkr-item>

  <bkkr-item>
    <bkkr-reorder slot="start"></bkkr-reorder>
    <bkkr-label>
      Item 4
    </bkkr-label>
  </bkkr-item>

  <!-- Custom reorder icon end items -->

    <bkkr-label>
      Item 5
    </bkkr-label>
    <bkkr-reorder slot="end">
      <bkkr-icon name="pizza"></bkkr-icon>
    </bkkr-reorder>
  </bkkr-item>

  <bkkr-item>
    <bkkr-label>
      Item 6

Properties‚Äã

disabled‚Äã

DescriptionIf true, the reorder will be hidden.
Attributedisabled
Typeboolean
Defaulttrue

Events‚Äã

NameDescription
bkkrItemReorderEvent that needs to be listened to in order to complete the reorder action.

Once the event has been emitted, the complete() method then needs to be called in order to finalize the reorder action. |

Methods‚Äã

complete‚Äã

DescriptionCompletes the reorder operation. Must be called by the bkkrItemReorder event.

If a list of items is passed, the list will be reordered and returned in the
proper order.

If no parameters are passed or if true is passed in, the reorder will complete
and the item will remain in the position it was dragged to. If false is passed,
the reorder will complete and the item will bounce back to its original position.
